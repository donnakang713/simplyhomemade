What is Simply Homemade?

As our society gets busier, preparing daily meals become a chore. Simply Homemade is a mobile application that makes finding a great meal super easy and affordable by connecting you with local cooks who want to share their passion for home cooking with the people in their community.

We are the next “Uber of home cooked meals” where users can buy and sell delicious meals with just a few taps on the screen. Anyone with a passion for cooking can apply to become a chef with our simple 3-step application process. Buying and selling homemade meals has never been this easy with Simply Homemade!

How Simply Homemade Works

Start exploring

Start exploring various meals being offered in your local area. Searching for the perfect homemade meal can be done with just a few taps on the screen with the Simply Homemade Mobile App. Sort by cuisine, price, location, categories, and date - so you get exactly what you want.

View details of your meal

Want to know more about your meal? You can view details of your meal, so that there are absolutely no surprises with your order. View all ingredients, reviews & ratings, images, and much more. Have questions? No worries! You can always contact the chef.

Want to become a chef?

Want to become a chef? Anyone with a passion for cooking can apply to become a chef with our simple 3-step application process.

Our chef dashboard makes it easy for chefs to run their side cooking business. Chefs can easily manage their meal postings, view messages, update their calendar, and view their business progress.

Simply enjoy

After you place a meal order, sit back and wait for your meal to be delivered! You can also track your order to see where your meal is, real-time.

Say goodbye to grocery shopping, cooking, and cleaning up. You're now free to do the things you love. Simply enjoy your fully prepared, home cooked meals at the comfort of your own home.
